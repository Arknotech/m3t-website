Qualitative Research

In-depth understanding of motivations, emotions, and human behavior through immersive techniques such as FGDs, IDIs, ethnography, and shop-alongs.

Overview

Qualitative research uncovers **motivations, perceptions, and emotional drivers** behind consumer decisions. Whether you need deep exploratory insights for concept development, product experiences, or brand perception, M3T provides end-to-end moderation, recruitment, analysis, and reporting.

Key Features

  • Focus Group Discussions (FGDs)
  • In-Depth Interviews (IDIs)
  • Ethnographic immersions
  • Shop-alongs & home visits
  • Projective techniques & stimulus testing

Typical Workflow

  1. Objectives alignment
  2. Screening & recruitment
  3. Discussion guide development
  4. Moderation & fieldwork
  5. Transcription & thematic coding
  6. Insight development & reporting

Deliverables

  • Full transcripts
  • Thematic coding matrix
  • Insight maps & behavior drivers
  • Video & audio recordings (upon request)
  • Concept evaluation insights
  • Final presentation deck
