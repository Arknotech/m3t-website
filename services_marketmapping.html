Market Mapping

Comprehensive mapping of outlets, distribution channels, and market territories to provide a complete view of business coverage and expansion opportunities.

Overview

Market Mapping provides companies a 360-degree understanding of their distribution universe. M3T identifies outlets, clusters, territories, and coverage gaps to help brands optimize sales routes, expansion strategy, and trade presence.

Key Features

  • Outlet census & geo-tagging
  • Trade clustering & segmentation
  • Coverage gap identification
  • Route planning and territory mapping
  • Competitor presence analysis

Typical Workflow

  1. Define mapping scope & universe
  2. Field deployment & outlet listing
  3. Geo-capturing & classification
  4. Data cleaning & deduplication
  5. Territory modeling
  6. Coverage maps & insights reporting

Deliverables

  • Outlet universe database
  • GIS-based coverage maps
  • Territory plans & route optimization reports
  • Competitor heatmap analysis
  • Market expansion recommendations
